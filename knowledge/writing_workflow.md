# My writing workflow

## Phase 1: Critical thinking

1. Gather information. First and most important, set up and test the feature or product when possible. Use secondary resources also: meeting minutes and recordings, planning documents, blogs, source code, etc.
1. Information audit. Do I really understand the feature or product I'm writing about? Don't make assumptions; if I'm uncertain, highlight the uncertainty and find the information before continuing.
